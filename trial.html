Project Description
Combating the spread of pests in Kenya(Locust) Locust have in the recent past made their way to Kenya from Somalia and are destroying livelihoods of the farmers.My project seeks to come up with measures to deal with the menace and to prevent more damage from the swarm which might lead to hunger in the country
Disease Control-COVID 19 COVID-19 is a disease caused by a newly identified coronavirus called SARS-CoV-2.It has been diagnosed in multiple locations worldwide and has since become a pandemic.My project seeks to track it's spread and try to come up with measures to prevent more spreading
